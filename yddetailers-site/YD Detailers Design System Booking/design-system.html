01 · Foundations

Voice & Principles

Black canvas, one decisive accent, hi-contrast type. The product comes to you — the design does the same: clear, confident, no clutter.

Confident

Short, direct copy. "Reserve Your Spot", not "Learn more and maybe book." Headings go to -1.5px tracking.

Premium

Deep black bg, generous space, pill buttons, soft glow on CTAs. Nothing looks cheap or busy.

Service-first

Mobile detailing means trust. Testimonials, before/afters, and comparison tables carry weight — lean into them.

14 · Known issues

Flagged for repair

⚠ Booking page is off-brand
The booking page uses Rajdhani + Montserrat + DM Mono with a slightly different blue (#4f7df3) and a gold-like hover state. Migrate it to Inter + --blue to unify brand.
⚠ Before/After slider has no JS
The live site's .ba-container renders handle markup but never attaches pointer/drag handlers. The slider is static. See the fixed version in section 09 — pointer + touch + hover.
⚠ Gallery slides reference missing assets
Slide images are empty src. Either embed base64 like the logo or point to real files in /images.
